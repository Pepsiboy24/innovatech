School Admin Portal:
- Dashboard with key statistics (total students, teachers, attendance rate, revenue)
- Navigation to Classes, Students, Teachers, Schedule, Time Table, Attendance, Fees
- Recent activity feed for monitoring actions (attendance reports, schedule updates, fee payments)
- Student management (add/view/search students)
- Teacher management (add/view/search teachers)
- Academic schedule management (calendar creation, uploading schedules)
- Time table management
- Attendance tracking
- Fee management and processing

Parents Portal:
- Home dashboard with child's academic overview
- Report Cards section with downloadable reports
- Payments section for fee payments
- Messages section for communication with teachers/admin
- Academic performance tracking (recent grades, overall progress chart)
- User profile with parent and child information

Students Portal:
- Dashboard displaying rank, points, badges, and achievements
- Classes section with scheduled upcoming classes and join class options
- Study Materials section providing learning resources
- Social section for student interactions and study groups
- AI Study Buddy for study-related AI assistance
- Class Leaderboard showing top-performing students
- Social Feed for posts and reactions

Teachers Portal:
- Dashboard with statistics for active courses, total students, upcoming classes, and messages
- Students management table with performance overview
- Attendance management and tracking
- Online Classes section
- AI Assistant for generating lesson notes, creating test questions, and analyzing student performance
- Messaging system for communication with students and parents
- Upcoming classes overview
